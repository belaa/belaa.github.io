About Me

I study observational cosmology, where I work on large ground-based surveys that aim to understand dark energy and its impact on the evolutionary history of the universe.

Awards and Honors

DOE SCGSR

The DOE Office of Science Graduate Student Research Award provides graduate students with supplemental funds to conduct part of their dissertation research at a host DOE laboratory. I carried out my six-month award term at SLAC National Laboratory, where I worked on Verification and Testing of the LSST Camera under the direction of Aaron Roodman.

Learn more

LSSTC Data Science Fellow

I participated in the LSSTC Data Science Fellowship Program from 2016-2018. This training program is designed to teach skills that are essential for dealing with big data in the era of LSST. Topics such as statistics, machine learning, image processing, databases and data visualization are taught over the course of this fellowship.

Learn more

Rudolph Minkowski Lick Observatory Workshop

University of California graduate students who are selected to participate in this workshop spend five days at Lick Observatory, where they learn a variety of topics related to observational astronomy, such as CCD astronomy and adaptive optics, in addition to having the opportunity to gain hands-on observing experience.

Learn more
